Time in the Zone

Convert and view time across multiple timezones

Query Parameter Usage

  • ?time=14:30 - Show specific time (uses today's date)
  • ?time=2025-12-25T14:30 - Show specific date and time
  • ?timezone=America/New_York - Change reference timezone
  • ?office=India - Use office location as reference (Portugal, India, Brazil, etc.)
  • ?time=14:30&office=Morocco - Combine time and office parameters

The "Nearest Office" badge shows based on your geolocation, regardless of the reference timezone.
